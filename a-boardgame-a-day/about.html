What is A Board Game A Day?

This blog is run by Hannah and John, a pair of avid board gamers. Between the two of us, we boast a collection of 80+ board games, including every edition of Love Letter released in the UK, every edition of Tanto Cuore and just about every edition of Ticket To Ride! One of our goals as a couple is to play at least one board game every day in 2018.

Whilst we're playing at least one game each day in 2018, we're keeping tabs on stats such as most played game, what percentage of our played games are co-operative, how many times John completes wipes the floor with Hannah in almost every versus game. Once a month, we'll be providing graphs, stats and facts about the games played in that month. This blog is where we'll be doing this.
